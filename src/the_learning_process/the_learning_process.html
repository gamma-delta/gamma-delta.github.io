The Learning Process

This webpage is intended to teach you about the learning process.

You will start out knowing absolutely nothing about the nature of these puzzles. By the end, however, you will be very good at them.

Then, you will discuss the way you got a handle on the puzzles with the rest of the class, and finally solve a puzzle together.
